Best Work

Growth Future

Out of all my work, this game encapsulates various skills essential in coding. Beyond coding, this project reflects my ability to partake in the planning process of coding by my ability to design a game from scratch. Additionally, my ability in executing my ideas can best be seen in this game, as I take pride in the simple yet engaging user interface that this game takes on. Although the basic coding of this game was easy, I went a step further in designing this game from being a simple side-scroller game by learning how to create two “pathways” that generate forever, have the obstacles speed towards the ghost, and regenerate and randomize the placement of the targets.
